Delivery Policy

At Oxford Sofa's, we take pride in delivering your premium furniture safely and on time. Please review our delivery policy to understand how we handle your orders.

Important: All deliveries require someone to be present at the delivery address to inspect and accept the goods. Please ensure someone is available during the scheduled delivery window.

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities and towns across India. Delivery availability will be confirmed during checkout.

2. Delivery Options

Standard Delivery

  • Delivery within 7-14 business days
  • Delivery window: 9AM-6PM
  • Ground floor delivery only
Free on orders above ₹20,000

Express Delivery

  • Delivery within 3-5 business days
  • Morning (9AM-1PM) or Afternoon (1PM-6PM) slots
  • Ground floor delivery only
₹1,500 or 5% of order value (whichever is higher)

Premium White Glove Service

  • Delivery within 5-7 business days
  • 2-hour delivery window
  • Room of choice delivery
  • Basic assembly included
  • Packaging removal
₹3,000 or 7% of order value (whichever is higher)

3. Delivery Process

  1. Order Confirmation: You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after purchase.
  2. Processing: We'll process your order within 1-2 business days.
  3. Dispatch Notification: You'll receive a dispatch notification with tracking details.
  4. Delivery Scheduling: Our delivery team will contact you 24-48 hours before delivery to schedule a convenient time.
  5. Delivery Day: Our team will deliver your items as scheduled.

4. Delivery Requirements

  • Someone must be present to accept and inspect the delivery
  • Clear access to the delivery location (hallways, doorways, staircases)
  • For white glove service, please clear the intended placement area
  • Have your order confirmation ready (digital or printed)

5. Delivery Timeframes

Standard delivery times are estimates and may vary due to:

  • Product availability
  • Customization requirements
  • Weather conditions
  • Logistical delays
  • Public holidays

6. Failed Deliveries

If delivery cannot be completed due to:

  • No one available to accept delivery
  • Incorrect address provided
  • Insufficient access to property

A redelivery fee of ₹1,000 will apply for standard delivery. Express and White Glove services will be charged at 50% of original delivery fee.

7. Delivery FAQ

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Yes, if your order hasn't been dispatched yet. Contact us immediately at support@oxfordsofas.com or call +91-XXXXXXXXXX. Address changes after dispatch may incur additional charges.

Do you deliver to apartments/high-rises?

Yes, but standard delivery only includes ground floor delivery. For upper floors, please select our White Glove service or additional floor delivery charges may apply.

What if my item arrives damaged?

Please refuse delivery of visibly damaged items. For concealed damage, notify us within 48 hours with photos for replacement arrangements.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes, you'll receive tracking information once your order is dispatched. You can also track through your account on our website.

  • Phone: +92-3198442632 (Delivery Hotline)
  • Hours: Mon-Sat, 8AM-8PM
