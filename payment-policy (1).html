Payment Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

Oxford Sofa's offers secure and convenient payment options for your furniture purchases. Please review our payment terms before completing your order.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

Credit Cards
Debit Cards
Net Banking
UPI Payments
EMI Options
Cash on Delivery*

*Cash on Delivery available for select products and locations only. Additional charges may apply.

2. Payment Security

We prioritize your payment security with:

  • SSL encrypted transactions
  • PCI DSS compliant payment gateways
  • Tokenization for card payments
  • 3D Secure authentication
  • No storage of sensitive payment data on our servers

3. Payment Terms

3.1 Standard Orders

  • Full payment required at time of purchase
  • Payment verification may take 1-2 business hours
  • Orders processed only after successful payment

3.2 Custom Orders

  • 50% advance payment required to begin production
  • Balance payment due before delivery
  • Customization changes after payment may incur additional charges

3.3 EMI Options

Bank Tenure Interest Rate Processing Fee
HDFC Bank 3-24 months 14% p.a. 1% of loan amount
ICICI Bank 3-18 months 15% p.a. 1.5% of loan amount
SBI Card 3-12 months 12% p.a. 1% of loan amount
Axis Bank 3-24 months 16% p.a. 1.25% of loan amount

*EMI terms subject to bank approval and may change without notice

4. Pricing & Currency

  • All prices in Indian Rupees (₹)
  • Inclusive of applicable GST
  • Exclusive of delivery charges unless specified
  • Prices subject to change without notice
  • Displayed prices prevail over printed catalogs

5. Refund Policy

  • Refunds processed within 7-10 business days of return approval
  • Issued to original payment method
  • EMI purchases refunded as per bank policies
  • Cash on Delivery refunds via bank transfer
  • See our Return Policy for details

6. Failed Transactions

If your payment fails but amount is deducted:

  1. Check with your bank for pending authorization
  2. Wait 2-4 hours for automatic reversal
  3. Contact us at payments@oxfordsofas.com if amount not reversed within 24 hours
  4. Provide transaction details for investigation

7. Payment Disputes

For any payment-related disputes:

  • Contact us within 7 days of transaction
  • Provide order and payment details
  • We will investigate and respond within 3 business days
  • Chargebacks without prior communication may result in account suspension

8. Contact Information
