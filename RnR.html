Return & Refund Policy

At Oxford Sofa's, we strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction with our premium quality sofas and furniture. Please review our return and refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

Note: Due to the customized nature of our products, certain items may have specific return conditions. Please check product details or contact our customer service for clarification.

1. Returns Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most standard products, provided:

  • The item is in its original condition (unused, unassembled, with all tags attached)
  • Original packaging is intact and undamaged
  • A valid proof of purchase is provided
  • The return is initiated within the specified timeframe

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless defective or damaged:

  • Customized or made-to-order sofas
  • Products marked as "Final Sale"
  • Assembled furniture (unless defective)
  • Clearance items

3. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage/defect
  • We will arrange for inspection and either repair, replacement, or refund

4. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service at returns@oxfordsofas.com or call +91-XXXXXXXXXX
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We will provide return authorization and instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Attach the provided return label
  6. Wait for confirmation of receipt and processing

5. Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • A restocking fee of 15% may apply for non-defective returns

6. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different color/fabric options (subject to availability)
  • Different models (price difference may apply)
  • Defective items (as described above)

Contact our customer service to arrange an exchange.

7. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement without penalty. For custom orders, cancellations must be made within 6 hours of order confirmation.

8. Contact Us

For any questions regarding our return and refund policy:

  • Email: support@oxfordsofas.com
  • Phone: +92 3198442632
  • Business Hours: Mon-Sat, 10AM-7PM
