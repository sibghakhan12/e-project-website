Sofa Care & Warranty Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

This policy covers warranty information and care instructions for your Oxford Sofa's furniture. Proper care will extend the life and beauty of your investment.

1. Warranty Coverage

Oxford Sofa's provides the following warranty for our premium sofas:

Component Warranty Period Coverage
Frame 5 Years Manufacturing defects in wooden/metal frame
Suspension 3 Years Springs and suspension systems
Cushion Foam 2 Years Abnormal sagging or deterioration
Fabric 1 Year Manufacturing defects in upholstery
Mechanisms 2 Years Recliner and sleeper mechanisms

2. Warranty Exclusions

The warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from misuse, accidents, or improper maintenance
  • Fading or discoloration from sunlight exposure
  • Stains or damage from spills
  • Modifications made by non-authorized personnel
  • Commercial or rental use

3. Care Instructions

Fabric Sofas

  • Vacuum weekly with upholstery attachment
  • Blot spills immediately with clean cloth
  • Professional cleaning recommended annually
  • Rotate cushions monthly

Leather Sofas

  • Dust weekly with dry, soft cloth
  • Use leather conditioner every 6 months
  • Keep away from direct heat sources
  • Avoid sharp objects

General Care

  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Maintain 40-70% humidity
  • Use coasters under drinks
  • Avoid sitting on arms

4. Warranty Claim Process

  1. Contact our customer service with your order details
  2. Provide photos/video of the issue
  3. We may arrange for inspection
  4. Approved claims will result in repair or replacement
  5. Transportation costs may apply for service visits

5. Professional Cleaning Recommendations

  • Fabric Sofas: Professional steam cleaning every 12-18 months
  • Leather Sofas: Professional conditioning every 12 months
  • Stain Removal: Use only manufacturer-approved cleaning products
  • Deep Cleaning: Recommended before warranty expiration

6. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean a stain on my fabric sofa?

Blot (don't rub) the stain with a clean, damp cloth. For tough stains, use a mild detergent solution. Always test cleaning products on an inconspicuous area first.

What if my leather sofa develops cracks?

Minor cracks can be treated with leather conditioner. For significant cracking within the warranty period, contact us for assessment.

Can I replace just one cushion?

Yes, we offer individual cushion replacements within the first year to ensure color matching. After one year, we recommend replacing all cushions for uniform appearance.
