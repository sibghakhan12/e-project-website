Installment Payment Policy

Last Updated: 10-07-2025

Oxford Sofa's offers flexible installment payment options to make your dream furniture more affordable. Please review our installment policy before choosing this payment method.

1. Installment Options

We offer the following installment plans:

1.1 Bank EMI Options

Bank Tenure Interest Rate Processing Fee Min. Order Value
HDFC Bank 3-24 months 14% p.a. 1% of loan amount ₹15,000
ICICI Bank 3-18 months 15% p.a. 1.5% of loan amount ₹10,000
SBI Card 3-12 months 12% p.a. 1% of loan amount ₹20,000
Axis Bank 3-24 months 16% p.a. 1.25% of loan amount ₹15,000

*Terms subject to bank approval and may change without notice

1.2 In-House Installment Plans

  • 3-Month Plan: 25% down payment, remaining split over 2 months (0% interest)
  • 6-Month Plan: 20% down payment, remaining split over 5 months (8% p.a. interest)
  • Custom Plans: Available for orders above ₹50,000 (contact our sales team)

*In-house plans require credit approval and valid ID proof

2. Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for installment payments:

  • Minimum order value of ₹10,000 (varies by bank)
  • Valid credit/debit card from partner bank
  • Sufficient credit limit
  • Indian resident with valid ID proof
  • For in-house plans: Credit approval required

3. Application Process

  1. Select EMI option at checkout
  2. Choose your preferred bank/tenure
  3. Complete bank authentication (OTP/Net Banking)
  4. Receive instant approval confirmation
  5. First EMI will be charged immediately (for most banks)

4. Important Terms

  • EMI conversion is at the sole discretion of your bank
  • All disputes regarding EMI charges must be resolved with your bank
  • Oxford Sofa's is not responsible for EMI interest/charges applied by banks
  • Order processing begins only after successful EMI approval
  • For in-house plans, delivery occurs after final payment

5. Cancellation & Returns

  • EMI cancellations must be processed through your bank
  • Returns follow standard Return Policy
  • Refunds for EMI purchases will be processed to your bank account
  • Interest charges are non-refundable
  • Pre-closure charges may apply (check with your bank)
