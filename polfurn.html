Furniture Policy

At Oxford Sofa's, we are committed to providing high-quality furniture and exceptional customer service. Please review our furniture policy below to understand our standards, warranties, and customer care procedures.

Quality Assurance

All Oxford Sofa's furniture is crafted with premium materials and undergoes rigorous quality control checks before leaving our facilities. We stand behind the quality of our products and offer the following assurances:

Materials & Construction

  • Frames constructed from kiln-dried hardwood for durability
  • High-density foam cushions with premium upholstery fabrics
  • Reinforced joints and stress points for long-term stability
  • Non-toxic, eco-friendly finishes and stains

All materials meet or exceed industry safety standards for furniture manufacturing.

Warranty Information

Oxford Sofa's offers comprehensive warranties to protect your investment:

Structural Warranty

10-year limited warranty on all frame components against manufacturing defects.

Upholstery Warranty

3-year limited warranty against fabric defects and seam failures.

Cushion Warranty

2-year limited warranty against excessive sagging or loss of resilience.

Warranty coverage begins from the date of delivery and requires proper maintenance and normal residential use.

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from accidents, abuse, or improper use
  • Normal wear and tear including fabric fading or minor cushion compression
  • Commercial or rental property use
  • Modifications made by non-authorized personnel

Delivery & Setup

We offer professional delivery and setup services to ensure your furniture arrives in perfect condition:

Delivery Process

  • White-glove delivery service available in all major metropolitan areas
  • Delivery team will place furniture in your desired room
  • Basic assembly included (for items requiring assembly)
  • Removal of packaging materials upon request

Inspection Requirements

Customers must inspect all items at the time of delivery and note any damage or defects on the delivery paperwork. Claims for damage discovered after signing must be made within 48 hours of delivery.

Care & Maintenance

Proper care will extend the life and beauty of your Oxford Sofa's furniture:

Fabric Care

  • Regular vacuuming with upholstery attachment
  • Immediate treatment of spills with clean, absorbent cloth
  • Professional cleaning recommended every 12-18 months

Leather Care

  • Dust regularly with soft, dry cloth
  • Condition with high-quality leather conditioner every 6 months
  • Keep away from direct heat sources and sunlight

Wood Care

  • Dust with microfiber cloth
  • Use furniture polish specifically formulated for wood finishes
  • Wipe spills immediately to prevent water marks

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Oxford Sofa's purchase:

Return Policy

  • 30-day return policy for unused items in original condition
  • Original packaging required for returns
  • Return shipping fees are the customer's responsibility
  • Custom or special order items cannot be returned

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges available within 14 days of delivery
  • Customer responsible for price differences and shipping fees
  • Exchange items must be in new, unused condition

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team at info@oxfordsofa.pk or call 021 111 178 762.
